Endless Possibilities:

From design intent, to an assembled prototype in minutes, not hours.

Design:

Out method uses a proprietary geometric solver to turn almost any network of lines, into a fabricable reciprocal frame.

Fabrication

Once the design is finalized, an in-house fabrication processes turns stock building elements into a kit of parts thats ready for assembly.

Assembly

Once the pieces are made, the frame is assembled in minutes, using a unique set of automatically generated assembly instruction.
