Reciproca

Rapid-prototyping for frame structures: design to assembly in minutes.

Nearly Endless Possibilities:

Reciprocal frames can be assembled from a kit of parts that isn't bound by preset connection angles. We are just beginning to explore the limits of this new design space.

Intuitive Design Tools

Our reciprocal solver takes care of the heavy lifting in the background, making it easy to learn and quick to iterate.

Integrated Fabrication Process

Our fabrication process turns stock linear elements into a kit of parts ready for assembly. Because of our unique roller design, stock lengths are not limited to machine size.

Assembly

Once the pieces are made, the frame is assembled in minutes, using a unique set of automatically generated assembly instruction.
